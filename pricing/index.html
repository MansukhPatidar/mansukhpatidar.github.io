FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do I need the hardware to use NoWires.AI?
No — the virtual breadboard works fully in the browser. Hardware is optional for physical sync.
Is the free plan really free?
Yes. No credit card required. The Free plan includes unlimited virtual projects forever.
What hardware does it support?
Currently the NoWires v1 board (ESP32-based). STM32 and third-party boards coming soon.
Can I use NoWires.AI for commercial projects?
Yes. All plans allow commercial use. Pro includes team collaboration features.
