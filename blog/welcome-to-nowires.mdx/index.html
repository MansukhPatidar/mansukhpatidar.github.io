Welcome to NoWires.AI

We’ve been quietly building something we think makers will love.

NoWires.AI is a virtual breadboard with a built-in AI circuit assistant. Design your circuit in the browser, validate it before touching hardware, and optionally sync it to our smart physical board over WiFi.

Why we built it

Every maker knows the frustration:

  • You’re prototyping late at night, breadboard wires everywhere
  • You accidentally short something and the magic smoke escapes
  • You spend 20 minutes reading a datasheet to figure out which way to orient a component
  • Your multimeter is in the other room

We wanted to fix all of that.

What we shipped

Phase 1: The virtual breadboard

A full 830-point breadboard in the browser. Accurate 5-hole strip connectivity, dual power rails, real component placement. Not a toy — this is the same electrical model you’d get on a physical board.

Phase 2: The AI assistant

Describe what you want to build. The AI understands your board state, suggests components and connections, explains errors in plain English, and guides you step by step.

Phase 3: Hardware sync

Connect our WiFi-enabled breadboard, press Sync, and your virtual design appears on the physical board. Voltage readings stream back live.

What’s next

We’re working on:

  • KiCad schematic export
  • More component definitions (sensors, displays, motor drivers)
  • Team collaboration features
  • Mobile app for live monitoring
